About LeaveTrack

Our mission is to simplify leave management for organizations of all sizes.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, LeaveTrack was born out of a need to modernize the way companies handle employee leave management. We noticed that many organizations were still using outdated spreadsheets or paper-based systems, leading to inefficiencies and errors.

Our team of HR professionals and software developers came together to create a solution that would save time, reduce administrative burden, and provide better visibility into workforce planning.

Key Features

Role-Based Access

Different access levels for employees, managers, and administrators ensure proper control and security.

Automated Notifications

Real-time alerts for leave requests, approvals, and reminders keep everyone informed.

Mobile Friendly

Access the system from any device, anywhere, making it convenient for both office and remote workers.
